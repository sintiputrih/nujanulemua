Emily VanCamp engaged to Josh Bowman; Soon to get married?

Emily VanCamp is a Canadian actress who made her name among the Television audiences as Amy Abbott on the TV series Everwood. Later she rose to stardom with the significant roles in big projects such as Revenge, Brothers & Sisters, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, and its sequel Captain America: Civil War.

Along with the career, Emily VanCamp’s dating affair with her boyfriend was also flourishing. The couple decided to take their dating affair to the next level and hence, got engaged last year. So, when will Emily VanCamp get married to her fiance? And, who is her fiance? Let’s explore the details along with her wiki-bio and net worth!

Emily VanCamp’s Early Life and a Glimpse on Her Career!

Emily VanCamp was born as Emily Irene VanCamp to parents Cindy and Robert VanCamp on 12 May 1986 in Port Perry, Ontario. Her father, Robert is an animal nutritionist. Emily’s first job was to assist her father, delivering food to clients. Growing up in an English- and French-speaking household, Emily Van Camp is fluently bilingual.

Emily’s childhood dream was different than she has now moved on to pursue. With a vision to become a professional dancer, she attended a summer training program in Montreal after convincing her parents. She had started studying dance since her very childhood, at the age of three.

Photo credit: sneakpeek.ca

Emily VanCamp began her acting career from Are You Afraid of the Dark? in 2000. However, she started gaining attention after she appeared as Amy Abbott in Everwood. Emily also received one Young Artist Awards nomination and four Teen Choice Awards nominations for her character ‘Amy.’ After several minor roles, Emily had her next big role on the ABC drama Brothers & Sisters where she cast as a regular during the show’s first four seasons.

Since then, Emily VanCamp has acted in movies and TV shows such as Norman, Captain America: The Winter Soldier, Boundaries, Revenge, and much more. Recently, Emily appears in FOX’s American drama series “The Resident” where she portrays the main role of Nicolette. You can watch her on the show “The Resident.”

Emily Van Camp Dating Affair; Secretly Married?

Actress Emily VanCamp has already got engaged with her longtime boyfriend, Josh Bowman. They are on the verge of getting married soon. But how was Emily VanCamp’s dating affair with her now-fiance, Josh Bowman? Let’s know the details:

Emily VanCamp met her now-fiance while filming ABC’s Revenge and soon started dating him. The two lovebirds soon became inseparable and announced their dating relationship in 2012. Gushing about her boyfriend, Emily told Women’s Health:

When I take off my makeup, that’s my boyfriend’s favorite. And that, to me, is a symbol of a great guy – someone who doesn’t care about all that [glamorous] stuff.

After dating for six years, Emily announced the news of her engagement via her Instagram account, flaunting a diamond ring on 11th May 2017.

Emily VanCamp: In Rush For Planning About Wedding?

Millions of fans are excited to hear the news of Emily VanCamp getting married to her fiance, Josh Bowman. However, it looks like they still have to wait longer as the engaged couple has yet to plan for their wedding. Yes, you heard it right! Emily is not in a rush for getting married and has yet to enter in the wedding planning zone.

At the winter 2018 Television Critics Association press tour, Emily VanCamp told PEOPLE:

We got engaged and then both hit the ground running with work.We’re not in a rush and we’re not in the wedding planning zone. I think once we start it’ll go quickly hopefully, but we’re both very focused on our work, so the little time we have together, we just want to be together.
