Bulldog Narrates How Kwaw Kese Linked Him To The Death Of Fennec Okyere

August 2024 · 2 minute read

The Chief Executive Officer of Bullhaus Entertainment, Lawrence Nana Asiamah Hanson, well known as Bulldog has revealed that Kwaw Kese reported him to the police following the demise of his manager, Fennec Okyere.

READ ALSO: Twene Jonas Goes Berserk On Ghanain Musician Who Brags About Their Wealth [Video]

Bulldog who was shocked by the move of Kwaw Kese revealed that he was arrested by the police within the premises of Accra-based Joy FM.

He recounted that the situation put him into a state of shock because he had no grudge with the late Fennec Okyere for him to eliminate him.

According to him, the police disclosed to him that the one who reported him was named Botchwey but per his findings, he got to know that Kwaw Kese was behind the arrest.

Speaking in an interview on Starr FM with Nana Aba Anamoah, Bulldog said; “The name of the person who reported me as the murderer was something something Botchway but I want you to know that it was Kwaw Kese who reported me as the murderer of Fennec Okyere.”

He revealed that the police promised to let him go just after the questioning so he had to drive them in his own car to the police station.

However, after the interrogation at the police station, the police said that he was to be locked up at Nsawam for being a prime suspect to the death of Fennec Okyere.

Although no investigation was conducted to ascertain their allegations, Bulldog said he was locked up and was told in the face that he killed the late Fennec Okyere.
