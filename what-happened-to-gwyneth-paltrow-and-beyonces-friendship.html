What Happened To Gwyneth Paltrow And Beyonces Friendship?

February 2024 · 4 minute read

Gwyneth Paltrow isn't everyone's cup of tea, as her promotion of certain products on Goop and her lavish lifestyle can be a turn-off for some. In recent years, the buzz surrounding the actress and businesswoman has been about her divorce from Chris Martin and her 2018 wedding.

Who does Paltrow hang out with if not everyone loves her? People have always said that she and Beyonce are super close, which is exciting to hear since it's not possible to think of two bigger stars. But in recent years, people have wondered if that still rings true. Let's investigate.

Becky With The Good Hair?

With the release of Beyonce's album Lemonade, fans wondered if Gwyneth Paltrow was "Becky with the good hair," the woman that Beyonce sang about in one song. As everyone remembers, this was supposedly the person that Jay-Z was having an affair with.

According to Cheat Sheet, Amber Rose appeared on a podcast and said, “I definitely think that Gwyneth Paltrow is ‘Becky with the good hair.' They were like friends, and then, like, you don’t see Gwyneth Paltrow with Beyoncé anymore. Now Gwyneth lost her husband, but like Beyoncé’s still with Jay." Paltrow's rep said that it was "completely absurd and 100 percent false."

Fans were even more curious about the status of this famous friendship when Paltrow married Brad Falchuk in 2018 and Beyonce wasn't there. People have said that Beyonce must have not been able to go because of her schedule, which does make sense.

Emails?

People say that the stars started fighting over emails in 2014. According to The Independent, Paltrow was hosting a charity sale on Goop. A source told Heat, "A while back, Bey had donated a pair of brightly colored stiletto boots (that designer Stuart Weitzman made just for her) to Gwyneth's charity sale on her Goop website. But, after the split, she thought it best that her name wasn't attached to the event."

The source continued, "Gwyn and Beyoncé haven't spoken much since Gwyn's split with Chris, although Bey has checked in on how she and the kids are doing."

Apparently, the problem was that Beyonce didn't want her name to be part of the charity sale, but Paltrow reportedly mentioned Beyonce's name.

Great Friends

Even though there has been a lot of chatter of Beyonce and Paltrow fighting and no longer being friends, they have only had the nicest things to say about each other over the years.

According to E Online, Paltrow had nothing but kind words for her pal in 2015 on Watch What Happens Live! She said, "The greatest perk of being their friend, is that I have a relationship with people who are also—who have similar...I mean, obviously I'm not as famous or successful as they are. But I do have a life in the public eye and I have people to sort of walk through life with in that capacity, and they're very kind and loving people. And it also means I get to be around their daughter."

Paltrow also shared that their families are close. Her son, Moses, calls Jay-Z "Uncle Jay" and has talked about wanting to "be like him." Her daughter, Apple, used to talk about singing like Beyonce when she was younger. In 2015, Paltrow talked about how much she enjoyed being able to spend time with Beyonce and Jay-Z's daughter Blue Ivy, according to Flare.com, so it definitely sounds like the families hang out a lot.

According to Buzzfeed, Beyonce said in 2011 that she loved being at Paltrow's home and she would want to just stay there. She said, "You go to her house and she makes you feel like you never want to go home. She is what I strive to be one day." The publication also mentioned that the couples would have double dates.

Whether or not the two superstars are still best friends, it's interesting to think back how they first met. According to Cheat Sheet, it was back in 2006 when they were attending an NYC charity event. The publication also notes that when Paltrow appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show in 2010, she said she and Chris Martin, Jay-Z, and Beyonce were "really close."

It sounds like even with a lot of talk about Beyonce and Gwyneth Paltrow fighting with one another, their friendship has lasted for years and doesn't show any sign of slowing down. At least that's what fans can hope, as fans of both stars love the idea of them hanging out all the time and getting their families together for casual dinners at Paltrow's beautiful home.
