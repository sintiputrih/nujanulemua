Giorgia Whigham- Biography

Giorgia Whigham is an actress best known for her role as Amy Bendix in the second season of “The Punisher.” She has also appeared in the Netflix series “13 Reasons Why” and will participate in the VH1 show “Scream.”

What is the Net Worth of Giorgia Whigham? Salary, Earnings

Sources estimate her net worth to be around $300,000 as of mid-2022, obtained through her so far brief but a successful acting career. Aside from her television job, she has also worked on various films, and it is believed that her wealth would grow as she continues her pursuits.

Giorgia Whigham- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Whigham was born on August 20, 1997, in New York City, USA. Giorgia’s life previous to her ascent to popularity is unknown, as she has not shared anything about her education or childhood. She is the daughter of actor Shea Whigham, best known for his role in the HBO series “Boardwalk Empire.” He also appeared in “Kong: Skull Island” and “Fast & Furious.”

Because of her father’s connections, she was able to earn her first acting role in the 2016 short film “Pinky,” in which she co-starred with Ellery Sprayberry. She obtained a lot more opportunities after her acting debut, including guest appearances on shows like “Shameless” and “The Orville,” as well as a recurring position on the show “Son of Zorn,” in which she voiced a character for the animated television series. The show was canceled after one season, but she rose to prominence after being cast in the web series “13 Reasons Why,” which airs on Netflix and is based on Jay Asher’s 2007 novel “Thirteen Reasons Why.” The plot revolves around a 17-year-old high school student who discovers a box of cassette records belonging to his deceased buddy Hannah Baker.

Giorgia Whigham- Relationship, Boyfriend

In terms of Whigham’s romantic connections, little is known about her personal life. She hasn’t mentioned having a boyfriend or dating anyone on her social media profiles, and she hasn’t been affiliated with anyone. Many of the cast members of “The Punisher,” including her co-star Jon Bernthal, remain close friends.

The Punisher’s Ascension to Notoriety

Following her debut in “13 Reasons Why,” Giorgia earned more acting opportunities in shows such as “The Orville” and “Animal Kingdom,” usually as a guest star, including a role as Teenage Denise in an episode of “Dirty John.” Her stardom skyrocketed in 2019 when she landed a starring part in the second season of Netflix’s “The Punisher.”

The show is situated in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and follows the same-named character. It is a spin-off of “Marvel’s Daredevil,” and it follows the story of Frank Castle, a vigilante known as “the Punisher,” who uses lethal tactics to eliminate criminals. Castle is portrayed by actor Jon Bernthal, and the show’s other cast members include Deborah Ann Woll, Ben Barnes, and Amber Rose Revah. Giorgia was introduced in the second season in a tale centered on her character, portraying a vagabond with a mysterious past whose adventures finally lead her to cross paths with Castle.

Projects

Following her performance in “The Punisher,” Giorgia continued to garner acclaim and was cast in a number of additional acting ventures. She landed a starring part in the third season of “Scream,” playing the character Beth. The show is an anthology series based on the same-named slasher film series, and its third season introduces a fresh cast of characters. MTV renewed the show for a third season, with worldwide rights going to Netflix after the first two seasons were set in the fictional town of Lakewood. A new showrunner would be introduced in a revival of the show, and she was cast alongside Tyga, RJ Cyler, Keke Palmer, and Jessica Sula.

She’s also expected to appear in the film “Saving Zoe,” which is based on Alyson Noel’s novel of the same name and stars her alongside Vanessa Marano and Laura Marano in lead roles and is set to be released in mid-2019 and directed by Jeffrey Hunt. She also appeared in the Netflix film “Sierra Burgess is a Loser,” in which she played the character Chrissy in a modern version of the “Cyrano de Bergerac” story starring Shannon Purser.

Social Media Details

She, like many other actors, is very active on social media, particularly on the websites Instagram and Twitter. She has almost 260,000 Instagram followers and frequently publishes personal images, typically with friends or other cast members from her numerous projects. Whigham also uses the site to advertise her acting credits, which include roles in “Scream,” “Saving Zoe,” and “The Punisher.” Her Twitter account, on the other hand, has over 5,000 followers and is rarely updated. Her final post on the account was a farewell to her character in “The Punisher,” which was discontinued earlier this year. She also uses the site to promote her job and provide updates on some of the news surrounding her time on “The Punisher.” She also shared images from her other projects, including “Saving Zoe.”
