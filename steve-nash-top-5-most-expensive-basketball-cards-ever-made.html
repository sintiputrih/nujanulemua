Steve Nash: Top 5 Most Expensive Basketball Cards Ever Made

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Iconic basketball legend Steve Nash has undoubtedly made his mark on the sport and in collectability circles for collectors of great cards by making passes, draining buckets; and helping to open not just doors but franchises like he did with the paradigm-roiling Brooklyn Nets. This article will take a look at the 5 most expensive Steve Nash basketball cards bought and sold through his career as an athlete -- along with insights into his top 5 cards.

  • 1997-98 Skybox Metal Universe Precious Metal Gems Green #101 Steve Nash (#002/100) – PSA EX 5

    • This card holds the distinction of being the second most expensive Steve Nash basketball card ever sold, commanding an astounding price of $120,000. Despite its PSA MINT 5 grade, which is relatively low in a market dominated by higher grades, this card's rarity and appeal have propelled its value to remarkable heights. With only 100 copies in existence, this card stands out as a prized possession for collectors.

  • 2009 Upper Deck Exquisite Dual Logoman Kobe Bryant & Steve Nash

    • Topping the list as the most expensive Steve Nash basketball card ever sold, this 2009 Upper Deck Exquisite Dual Logoman featuring Kobe Bryant and Steve Nash fetched an astonishing $156,000. This unique card, graded at 8.5 PSA MINT, showcases the convergence of two basketball icons, adding an intriguing layer of historical significance to its value.

  • 1996 Topps Chrome Refractor Steve Nash ROOKIE #182 PSA 10 GEM MINT

    • The 1996 Topps Chrome Refractor Steve Nash Rookie Card, graded at PSA 10 GEM MINT, holds its ground as a coveted piece among collectors. The pristine condition of this card, coupled with its significance as Nash's rookie card, has contributed to its notable value in the market.

  • 2005-06 UD Exquisite Collection Patch Parallel Autograph #32-AP Steve Nash Signed Game-Used Patch Card (#1/1) – BGS NM-MT+ 8.5/Beckett 10

    • This autographed game-used patch card from the 2005-06 UD Exquisite Collection, featuring Steve Nash, is a rare gem in the world of basketball memorabilia. With only one of its kind in existence, this card's unique attributes and the allure of an autographed game-used patch have propelled it to a significant value in the market.

  • 1997 Fleer Metal Precious Gems #101 Steve Nash #43/100

    • Rounding up the top 5, the 1997 Fleer Metal Precious Gems Steve Nash card, limited to 100 copies, has garnered substantial attention from collectors. Its limited availability and the allure of the "Precious Gems" series have contributed to its notable position among the most valuable Steve Nash basketball cards.

    • How to Uncover the Value of Basketball Cards

    The value of basketball cards, including those of Steve Nash, is influenced by a myriad of factors, including rarity, condition, historical significance, and market demand. For collectors and enthusiasts seeking to appraise the value of basketball cards, several avenues exist to gain insights into the dynamic world of sports memorabilia:

    Authentication and Grading Services: Organizations such as PSA and Beckett offer authentication and grading services, providing collectors with valuable assessments of a card's condition and authenticity.Auction Houses and Online Marketplaces: Monitoring auction results and sales on reputable platforms like eBay, PWCC Marketplace, and Heritage Auctions can offer valuable insights into the current market value of basketball cards. Engaging with Collecting Communities: Participating in forums, social media groups, and attending sports memorabilia shows can provide collectors with opportunities to engage with fellow enthusiasts and gain valuable knowledge about the evolving market trends.

    In the End

    In conclusion, the world of basketball card collecting offers a captivating blend of history, rarity, and market dynamics, as evidenced by the impressive values commanded by the top 5 most expensive Steve Nash basketball cards. Whether you're a seasoned collector or a newcomer to the hobby, the allure of these prized possessions continues to captivate the hearts and minds of sports memorabilia enthusiasts worldwide.
