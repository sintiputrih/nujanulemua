Daniel John Haren, born on September 17, 1980, in Monterey Park, California, is a former professional baseball pitcher who began his career in the Major League Baseball (MLB) after being drafted by the St. Louis Cardinals in the 2001 MLB June Amateur Draft from Pepperdine University. Early Success and Recognition

Haren made his MLB debut on June 30, 2003, and went on to have a successful career spanning several years. His early years in the league saw him achieve significant milestones, including being an All-Star pitcher and making significant contributions to the teams he played for.
