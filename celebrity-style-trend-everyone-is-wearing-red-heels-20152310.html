Celebrity Style Trend: Everyone Is Wearing Red Heels!

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Calling all Wizard of Oz fanatics — this is a trend for you! Hollywood’s A-list ladies have been stepping out in style, and it’s all thanks to the incorporation of one fierce classic: the red shoe.

Feast your eyes on these beauts in fabulous crimson footwear! Rachel Bilson paired hers with a floral dress, Lupita Nyong’o went head-to-toe red, and Reese Witherspoon jazzed up her jeans with cherry pumps, and that’s just the start.

Scroll down to see everyone who wore red shoes this past month!

America Ferrera

The Ugly Betty alum, 31, added a pop of sizzle to her blue separates with red Kurt Geiger heels while attending the 22nd Annual Elle Women in Hollywood Awards on Oct. 19, in Beverly Hills.

Abigail Spencer

The True Detective actress, 34, contrasted her shamrock-hued Monique Lhuillier lace dress with red pumps at the 11th annual LA Femme International Film Festival Awards Gala in Los Angeles on Oct. 18. 

Tatiana Maslany

The Orphan Black actress, 30, rocked a cinched black jumpsuit with suede Kurt Geiger stilettos at a PaleyFest screening of her hit series in NYC on Oct. 18.

Rachel Bilson

The Hart of Dixie alum, 34, slipped into ruby Paul Andrew heels for the No Kid Hungry Benefit Dinner in L.A. on Oct. 14.

Zooey Deutch

Also at the No Kid Hungry event, the Vampire Academy actress, 20, channeled her inner Reese at the Globes circa 2007 in a canary yellow dress and red shoes.

Lupita Nyong’o

The Oscar-winning actress, 32, modeled an all-red ensemble, including Aquazzura pumps, at the opening night celebration of her NYC stage debut, Eclipsed, on Oct. 14.

Lady Gaga

Putting a fall spin on white after Labor Day, the American Horror Story: Hotel actress, 29, teamed her red-striped LWD with a red hat and matching Brian Atwood shoes while strolling through LAX on Oct. 13.

Olivia Wilde

The mom to baby Otis added even more whimsy to her floral-cutout halter dress with red shoes by Paul Andrew at the NYC premiere of Meadowland on Oct. 11.

Reese Witherspoon

The Draper James founder, 39, finished her street style ensemble with leopard-print cherry pumps on Sept. 30 in L.A.
