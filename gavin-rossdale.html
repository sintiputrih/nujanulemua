Gavin Rossdale | Girlfriend, Children, Wife, Net Worth, Gwen Stefani, Daughter, Daisy Lowe

January 2024 · 5 minute read

Gavin McGregor Rossdale was born on 30th October 1965 in Marylebone, London, to parents Barbara Stephan (née Bowie) and Douglas Rossdale. His father was a Russian-Jewish doctor while her mother was Scottish. Their family name was originally Rosenthal.

Rossdale was the lead singer of the rock band Bush from 1992 till 2002. Following the band's separation, he became the lead singer and guitarist for the American rock band Institute. After releasing the band's only album Distort Yourself in 2005, he strived towards a solo career.

In 2010, Gavin's initial band Bush reunited, and he subsequently took over his original role. Three years later, he was awarded the Ivor Novello Award by BASCA for International Achievement.

Gavin Rossdale was married to his former wife Gwen Stefani

Gavin Rossdale with his ex-wife Gwen Stefani.

Rossdale met fellow singer Gwen Stefani back in 1995 when their respective bands, Gavin's 'Bush' and Gwen's 'No Doubt', toured together.

Rossdale and his girlfriend Stefani were in a relationship for a while before taking a break. During this period, the singer began dating Courtney Love and did so for eight months in 1995-96. Kurt Cobain's widow Courtney said, "Everyone gave me so much shit because Gavin sounded a lot like Kurt."

Fast forward to 2002, Gavin married Stefani. The duo subsequently became parents to three children, all of whom are sons. Their first son Kingston James McGregor Rossdale, was born on May 2006. He was followed by a couple more kids - Zuma Nesta Rock Rossdale and Apollo Bowie Flynn Rossdale on August 2008 and February 2014, respectively.

A little over a year later, Rossdale's wife filed for divorce on 3rd August 2015, citing 'irreconcilable differences.' The pending divorce was finalized nine months later on April 2016.

The singer is the biological father of Daisy Lowe

Rossdale with his daughter Daisy Lowe from his relationship with Pearl Lowe.
Source: Daily Mail

Gavin Rossdale is the father of the English fashion model Daisy Rebecca Lowe born in 1989. This was concluded following a paternity test conducted in 2004.

Daisy was born to her mother Pearl Lowe, who had a brief relationship with Daisy's longtime godfather Gavin Rossdale. Sometime after her birth, Gavin cut off all contacts with the mother-daughter duo.

A year later, Rossdale revealed his relationship with Daisy is more like a 'friendship.' In fact, both of them admitted they carry a good relationship that is based on respect for one another.

How rich is Gavin Rossdale?

Gavin Rossdale's net worth is estimated to be $35 million.
Source: Spear's Magazine

Rossdale has made most of his earnings through his career as a rhythm guitarist and singer. His 1994 album Sixteen Stone with the rock band Bush earned $6.8 million. Likewise, Razorblade Suitcase (1996) and The Science of Things (1999) made $3.6 million and $1.1 million, respectively.

Following the band's reunion in 2010, they released the album The Sea of Memories (2011), which made a measly $20,000.

Besides his career as a music artist, Gavin has acted in a few movies too. His most notable appearances in the big screens include Constantine (2005), How to Rob a Bank (2008), and The Bling Ring (2013).

As of 2019, Rossdale's net worth is estimated to be $35 million.
