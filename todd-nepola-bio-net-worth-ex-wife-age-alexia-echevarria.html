How Much Is Todd Nepola Net Worth?

Todd Nepola should have a net worth of at least $10 million. He is the founder – president – CEO of Current Capital Real Estate Group. He has been running the company since March 2002.

According to the bio on his company’s website, Todd is responsible for the company’s vision and long-term strategic plans. He follows three generations of real estate investors and developers of commercial real estate.

Todd graduated from the University of South Florida in 1994. While pursuing a career in Investment Banking, he followed in his family’s footsteps and started acquiring investment properties and developing properties for himself. After a decade of buying, building, leasing, and managing his properties, Todd decided to open up his firm.

With properties throughout Florida, Todd has opened a second office in Port St Lucie, FL to be even more accessible. He has been recognized as a winner of the CoStars 2014 “Power Broker” award, in addition to being a member of the international council of shopping centers (ICSC). With over two decades of “hands-on” experience in acquisitions, development, leasing, and management of commercial real estate, Todd proudly leads Current Capital Group.
