La La Anthony Files A Divorce From Carmelo Anthony

August 2024 · 6 minute read

La La Anthony is an American television personality, author, businesswoman, producer, and actress. She is also a famous disc jockey for MTV TRL and reality star known for her VH1 series LaLa’s Full Court Life and worked as a VJ for Total Request Live.

Recently, she has emerged in the news headlines after her divorce filing with the NBA star Carmelo Anthony.

La La Anthony Files A Divorce From Carmelo Anthony

La La and Carmelo Anthony’s has had a course of rocky relationship over the years together. Most of the time, they have been through it together, but now they’ve decided to call it quits officially.

La La filed divorce documents on 17 June 2021 in New York, 11 years after tying the knot with the NBA star, and cited irreconcilable differences.

La La Anthony and her ex-husband Carmelo Anthony (©: ahzabnews.com)

Sources close to the former couple tell TMZ that they have been separated for a while but remain friends, as always, after 16 years together, and everything about the divorce is amicable. The two have a 14-year-old son, Kiyan, together.

The two have had an on-and0off relationship over the past couple of years. They separated in 2017 when the rumors Carmelo cheated on her surfaced, but by late 2018 they seemed to have worked it out and had a happy relationship.

But now, they seemed to have had a strained relationship even after reuniting since the cheating rumors from Carmelo’s side. And finally, La La has forwarded for their separation, filing a divorce from him.

La La Anthony Relationship Details

La La has recently departed from a decade-long relationship with the NBA star Carmelo Anthony.

Carmelo Anthony is an American professional basketball player for the Portland Trail Blazers of the National Basketball Association. He has been a notable competitor among individual NBA players over the years.

Both of them was rising star when they first linked through a mutual friend. They started as a friend and their relationship evolved. They became an inseparable couple from the start.

La La and Carmelo started dating in the early 2000s and got engaged on Christmas Day of 2004. They welcomed their son, Kiyan Carmelo Anthony, in March of 2007.

The pair tied the knot six years after their engagement at Cipriani’s New York in 2010. The wedding ceremony was filmed by VH1 and aired as part of a reality series based on the couple that was named Lala’s Full Court Wedding.

They were having a nice time in their relationship until 2012, which was a turning point for them. Carmelo was publicly accused of cheating on his wife after a 19-year-old woman claimed she had a two-month relationship with him after he messaged her on Twitter. Then, in 2013 they even led to rumors of marital problems, and the couple lived apart for two months.

Years later, La La went on to be accused of cheating with rapper Maino. A woman claiming to be Maino’s girlfriend even thrashed out to La La through social media.

Eventually, the ex-couple took to social media to kill the rumors by rapping Nicki Minaj and Meek Mill’s song, All Eyes On You.

Nonetheless, the pair got even farther from each other when another infidelity rumor surfaced from Carmelo’s side. Carmelo reportedly impregnated a woman other than his then-wife, which made its headlines at the time. This resulted in La La and Carmelo separating briefly after April 2017 until they reconciled in late 2018. However, they now have decided to end their relationship for good amicably.

Who Is La La Anthony?

La La Anthony was born on 25 June 1979, in Brooklyn, New York, the USA, as Alani Nicole Vazquez.

Information about her parents is not disclosed as of now. But on the ethnic side, she is of Puerto Rican descent. She has two younger sisters, Aviana and Solana, and one younger brother named Christian.

As a young child, she was involved in many activities, but the music was her main interest. She went to Howard University, where she studied communications but did not graduate.

The actress stands at the height of 5 ft 6 in or 1.68 m. She has a decent weight with a slim body appearance.

Net Worth And Career Details

With over a decade of effort in her professional career, she has accumulated a fortune of wealth for herself. According to CelebrityNetWorth, she has an estimated net worth of $30 million.

Her career started at 15 when she entered for work in radio at WHAT-FM HOT 97.5 in Georgia. She also co-hosted her own radio show, The B-Syde, later on. Then she joined the MTV family.

La La is well known for her power in Power, alongside her former work as an MTV VJ on Total Request Live, as the host of reality television shows such as Flavor of Love and I Love New York.

From the early 2000s, she started her own production company 'Krossover Productions' and has continued to involve in the production teams for several TV shows and films. She worked as a producer on Mike Tyson’s documentary, Tyson, and produced a feature film with director/producer Brett Ratner.

La La has also appeared as an actress in films Two Can Play That Game, Think Like a Man, Baggage Claim and television shoes NYC 22, Single Ladies, and such. Other film credits include Urban Massacre, Monster Island, and You Got Served, to name a few.

In October 2011, she made her stage debut in the off-Broadway production of Love Loss and What I Wore. She has also co-produced Eclipsed on Broadway, starring Oscar winner Lupita Nyong’o. She also starred in, and executive produced five seasons of La La’s Full Court Wedding, which featured her wedding ceremony with Carmelo Anthony.

In February 2012, she launched a cosmetic line named MOTIVES by La La. In 2013, she created a clothing line, 5th & Mercer, a capsule collection exclusive to Shopbop.com, and she continues to work on new designs for the line. She also emerged as an author with her debut book titled The Love Playbook in January 2014. She then released her second book, entitled The Power Playbook, which was released in May 2015.

In October 2019, La La launched her clothing line La La Anthony Collection online. As for now, she does not seem to be quite active in the entertainment industry, but it is still not the end for her. So fans can look forward to her on-screen appearances.
