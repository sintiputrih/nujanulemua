Who Is Holly Madison Dating? Heres A Look At The TV Personalitys Love Life And Her Past Relati

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Holly Madison, the American television personality best known for her role in the TV series The Girls Next Door, is buzzing as a result of fans’ renewed interest in her romantic life. If this describes you, you’ve come to the right place to learn about Madison’s love life!

Who Is Holly Madison Dating?

As Of January 2023, Holly Madison Is Not Dating Anyone. The TV personality, who is also popular as Hugh Hefner’s ex-girlfriend, apparently has not given any update on her love life.

No indications of her dating anyone can be seen on any of her social media profiles either. As per reports, Madison hasn’t been dating anyone since divorcing the businessman Pasquale Rotella.

A Look At Madison’s Past Relationships!

Madison And Pasquale Rotella: 2013-2019

According to many reports, Pasquale Rotella, a businessman and the CEO of Insomniac, was Madison’s most recent partner. Madison and Pasquale are the parents of two children.

The former couple were married in 2013, but eventually got divorced because their personalities weren’t getting along.

In an interview with Radar Online, Pasquale’s father revealed the reason for Madison and his son’s divorce:

“Sexually and emotionally they were not getting along, they just have differences in what they are going after in life.”

Madison And Jack Barakat: 2010-2011

Madison has been in a relationship with Jack for not more than a year, from 2010 to 2011. Her relationship with the guitarist didn’t last long, unfortunately.

Madison And Criss Angel: 2008-2009

Madison and Criss Angel also had a short-lived relationship. Madison was in a relationship with the American magician from 2008 to 2009. According to Daily Mail, she penned down the reason for her breakup with Criss:

“For all his fame, fortune, and success, Criss, to me, seemed cripplingly insecure,” Holly Madison wrote.

Holly Madison even explained Criss’s domineering behavior, claiming that he cut her off from her friends, pressured her to quit her job, and became envious of her fame.

Madison And Hugh Hefner: 2001-2008

Out of all of Madison’s exes, Hugh Hefner is the most popular one. Hefner is well known for his TV shows such as The Girls Next Door and The American Playboy, to name a few. American magazine publisher Hugh Hefner is also popular for his controversies.

He was the creator and editor-in-chief of Playboy, a magazine that attracted accusations of obscenity for its provocative images and stories. The Playboy brand was expanded by Hefner into a global network of Playboy Clubs.

Facing credit card debt and potential homelessness, in August 2001, Madison moved into the Playboy Mansion and officially became one of Hugh Hefner’s girlfriends.

Madison has stated that as she looks back on her time at the mansion, her feelings for Hefner were caused by Stockholm syndrome. As a result, on October 7, 2008, Madison announced that her relationship with Hefner had ended.

What Is The Podcast That Madison Is Currently Streaming?

Holly Madison, the mother of two, is currently streaming her own podcast, Girls Next Level, alongside Bridget Marquardt, who was also a part of the TV series The Girls Next Door. The ex-girlfriends of Hefner are streaming their podcast on both Spotify and Apple Podcast platforms and have streamed 23 episodes as of January 2023.

To clarify what was real and what was phony, the girls take you inside the walls of the Playboy Mansion and behind the scenes of the popular show. Girls Next Level will be bringing you everything from the wild parties to the backstabbing drama, lifetime friendships, and tangled relationships.
