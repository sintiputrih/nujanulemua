Nikki Bella Net Worth, Age, Bio, Twitter, and Instagram

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Bella is an American professional wrestler and TV personality known for competing in the WWE alongside her twin sister. The Nikki Bella net worth figure is $7 million as of 2022.

Early Life and Bio

Stephanie Nicole Garcia-Colace was born in November of 1983 in San Diego, California to parents Jon and Kathy. Notably, she is of Mexican and Italian descent.

Meanwhile, Bella was raised on a farm in Scottsdale, Arizona alongside her identical twin Brie and her brother JJ. As far as education goes, Nikki graduated from Chaparral High School in 2002.

Afterward, she enrolled at Grossmont College in California to play soccer. However, she moved to Los Angeles after one year to try and find a talent agent.

Eventually, Bella began modeling and acting. In fact, her on-screen debut came on the Fox reality show Meet My Folks. In 2006, she and her sister missed the WWE Diva Search cut.

Nikki Bella Net Worth and Career

The Bella twins signed a developmental contract with the WWE in 2007. Then, the sisters were drafted to the Raw brand as part of the 2009 supplemental draft. Now, Nikki is the highest-paid women’s WWE star, earning $400 thousand per annum according to Sportskeeda.

Nikki ended her in-ring career in 2019 after a cyst was found on her brain. However, she is still working with the promotion as an ambassador. On top of that, she was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2020.

Outside of the ring, Bella has appeared on television in shows like Ridiculousness, Total Divas, Psych, Total Bellas, Dancing with the Stars, Celebrity Ninja Warrior, and The Bachelorette.

Nikki was a judge on America’s Got Talent: Extreme with Simon Cowell and Travis Pastrana.

Her other endeavors include launching a YouTube channel, two wine labels, a beauty line, a baby care line, a podcast, penning a memoir, and appearing in music videos.

Nikki Bella Instagram and Twitter

Currently, the pro wrestler possesses a whopping 10.5 million Instagram followers. To go along with that, Bella has another 3.5 million followers on her Twitter account as of July 2022.

Personal Life

Originally, Bella married her high school sweetheart in 2003, but it lasted merely three years.

Then, she started dating WWE superstar John Cena in 2012 and got engaged in 2017. Yet, the couple canceled their wedding one month in advance and decided to separate.

Bella has been dating her Dancing with the Stars partner since 2019. Now, the couple is engaged and they have a son by the name of Matteo Artemovich Chigvintsev.

Nikki Bella Net Worth

The Nikki Bella net worth figure of $7 million has been amassed since joining the WWE’s official roster in 2007. Over that span, Nikki has evolved into the wealthiest women’s pro wrestler on earth. She appears on other shows and releases her own products as well.
