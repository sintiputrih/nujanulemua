“any given sunday” is the opening track of Kehlani’s album “blue water road,” and it sets the tone for the record. The song features guest vocals from Blxst, an LA rapper known for his adlibs. In this moody and laid-back cut, both Kehlani and Blxst express their longing for their partners and reminisce about their experiences at Miami strip clubs.

What makes “any given sunday” significant is that it marks Kehlani’s first song since publicly identifying as a lesbian. In the lyrics, she embraces a more masculine identity with lines like “Call me daddy in front of all your bitches in the lobby.” This moment in the song showcases her willingness to explore and express her authentic self through her music.

Personally, “any given sunday” holds a special place in my heart. As someone who has also gone through a journey of self-discovery and changing identities, I resonate with Kehlani’s openness and courage to share her truth with the world. The song’s introspective lyrics and sensual vibes create a space for introspection and self-expression.

The collaboration between Kehlani and Blxst adds a unique dynamic to the song. Their voices blend effortlessly, creating a captivating atmosphere that draws listeners in. The melodic flow and smooth production elevate the overall listening experience, making “any given sunday” a standout track on the album.

Listening to “any given sunday” feels like entering a dreamy realm where emotions and desires intertwine. The poetic lyrics and seductive undertones create a captivating narrative that invites listeners to explore their own fantasies and desires. The song encourages self-expression and the embracing of one’s true identity, regardless of societal norms or expectations.

Kehlani’s ability to convey emotions through her music is truly exceptional. Her vulnerability and authenticity shine through, making her relatable and inspiring for her listeners. “any given sunday” serves as a testament to the power of music as a form of self-expression and liberation.

Overall, “any given sunday” by Kehlani is a mesmerizing and introspective track that sets the tone for her album “blue water road.” It explores themes of identity, desire, and self-expression, inviting listeners to embark on a journey of introspection and self-discovery. Kehlani’s willingness to share her personal experiences and embrace her true self makes this song a powerful and meaningful addition to her discography.

I encourage you to listen to “any given sunday” with an open mind and let the music guide you on your own journey of self-discovery and authenticity.
