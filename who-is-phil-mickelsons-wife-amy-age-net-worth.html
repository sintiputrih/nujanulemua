Who is Phil Mickelsons wife Amy, Age, Net Worth?

February 2024 · 3 minute read

PHIL MICKELSON, nicknamed “Lefty,” is a professional golfer from the United States who has won multiple major tournaments and is expected to compete in The Open in 2022.

Mickelson is one of just 12 players in golf history to have won three of the four majors. And he’ll be fighting for another one at The Open this week.

How old is Phil Mickelson?

Mickelson was born in 1970 and is 52 years old.

He became pro in 1992 and has since gone on to become one of America’s finest players.

Several times, he was rated second in the world by the Official World Golf Ranking.

The American won the PGA Championship at the age of 50 years and 11 months, becoming him the oldest man to win a major golf tournament.

What is his net worth?

According to Deemples, Mickelson is worth $400 million in 2021.

He is one of the wealthiest golfers in the world.

Mickelson’s $91 million in career prize money trails only Tiger Woods.

In 2020, he signed sponsorship partnerships with Amstel Light and hat maker Melin.

Who is Phil Mickelson’s wife Amy Mickelson?

Amy Mickelson was born Amy McBride in 1972.

She met Phil in 1992 at Arizona State University yet was far from star struck when he told her he was a professional golfer.

In fact, she thought he meant that he worked at the golf course shop!

The couple went for their first date on a tennis court and the rest is history.

At the time, Phil was a senior and Amy was a junior as well as a cheerleader on the Phoenix Suns NBA cheerleading squad.

In 2009 Amy was diagnosed with breast cancer and Phil put a hold on his PGA Tour on hold to help care for her.

She returned to golf in 2010, after her husband won his third Green Jacket at the Masters.

Amy conquered cancer after an 11-month struggle and now has a clear bill of health.

Amy has been highly involved in fund-raising activities for breast cancer research as a result of her near shave.

Phil Mickelson has how many children?

Three, Amanda was born the day after Phil finished second to Payne Stewart in the 1999 US Open. Sophia was born in 2001, followed by Evan two years later in 2003.

Amy burst an artery and Evan was not breathing for a long due to delivery problems, but both survived.
