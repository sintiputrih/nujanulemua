Vedika Pinto Biography

 Vedika was born on 14th October 1995 in Mumbai in a Christian family. Her father Johnny Pinto worked as an associate in the Hindi film industry and her mother Monia Saigal Pinto is a banker by profession. Pinto completed her early education at Ecole Mondiale Worl School in Mumbai. Later she enrolled in the University of London for her higher education. She has a brother Veer Pinto, he also lives in Mumbai.
