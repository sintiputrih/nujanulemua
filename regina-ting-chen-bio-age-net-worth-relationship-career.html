How did  Ting Chen start her Professional Career?

Regina Ting Chen made her acting debut in the 2013 short drama Burning Sky. The next year, she worked as a voiceover in the animation TV series SoniAni: Super Sonico the Animation.

She also worked on The Song, Extinction Event, American Dynasty, Fool’s Gold, The Game, and A Promising Game in her early career. In the 2017 television series Dreamsville, she played Alice.

She starred as Noelle in the feature film Lion Killer and as Crew Chief in the miniseries Watchmen in 2019. She also starred as a guest star in the TV programs The Outsider and The Resident in the year 2020.

In 2021, she featured in The Falcon and the Winter Soldier, a TV miniseries, and the film Conjuring: The Devil Made Me Do It.

Ting Chen is now appearing as Catherine Nakai in the television series Queen of the South. Similarly, she co-stars as Ms. Kelly in the Netflix series Stranger Things with Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Finn Wolfhard, and others.
