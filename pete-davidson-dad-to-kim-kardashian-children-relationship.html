How Did Pete Davidson Feel About Potentially Being A Stepdad?

To anyone paying attention to the situation, it was pretty obvious that Pete would have loved being a stepfather to Kim's kids.

Pete and Kim's kids spent a lot of time together, but there was a lot of preparation that happened before they met each other.

Kim was very conscious of how her divorce from Kanye could affect her kids, so she planned ahead of Pete meeting her children. According to an interview Kim did with the Today show, she revealed that she consulted with therapists before the kids met Pete. She also waited for her and Pete's relationship to get serious, too. Then, Pete and Kim's kids actually met. It's safe to say they definitely got along.

While Pete never publicly said anything about Kim's kids, some rumors from an important source can definitely tell a story. According to People, a source close to Kim said that both Kim and Pete were planning on having kids together. That source also claimed that Pete "loved having the kids around". There's also the tattoos that Pete got on his neck that spell the initials of Kim's four kids.

Combining all of that with the heavy rumors that Kim and Pete were planning on settling down for something long-term, it's likely that Pete wondered what it would be like to be the stepdad of Kim's kids. Given the fact that he loved her kids and her kids loved him, Pete probably loved the idea of becoming a permanent part of their family.
