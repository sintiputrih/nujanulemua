Not all bras are created equal! It can be challenging to find one without an underwire that stabs your side or one with straps thick enough to hold your girls up. However, thanks to Intimissimi’s new styles and sizes, your search is about to get a whole lot easier.

The Italian clothing label just released four new bras in larger cups for the brand’s Vera Collection. The cup sizes include B, C, D, DD and F, while the underbust includes measurements of 34, 36, 38, 40 and 42. If you’re excited to try one of the new lingerie pieces and want full coverage with an elegant style, then try the Mina Lace Balconette Bra.

This $79 design has a wraparound cup with a soft cotton interior so your skin won’t be irritated by the lace. The straps are also adjustable and have different widths based on the cup size for extra support. You can grab this bra in two neutral colors: black and a shade of nude.
