Alisson Becker biography

Álisson Ramsés Becker, generally known as Alisson, is a professional Brazilian footb aller who plays for Liverpool.

The 30-year-old goalkeeper started his career in 2013 when he joined Internacional in the Brazilian Série A. He spent 4 seasons with the club and made a total of 115 appearances which impressed the Italian club, AS Roma.

Alisson joined AS Roma in 2016-2017. He made a lot of fame for himself during his second season at Roma which happened to be his last.

He helped take AS Roma to the 2017-2018 UEFA Champions League Semi-Finals, his performance was very impressive and caught the eyes of the English Premier League giants, Liverpool.

He joined Liverpool in 2018-2019 and has since then been with the Premier League side for 5 good seasons making 204 appearances so far and managing to score a goal as a goalkeeper.

How old is Alisson Becker?

Alisson Becker was born in Hovo Hamburgo which is a city in Brazil. He was born on 2nd October 1992 and is currently 30 years old.

What is Alisson Becker’s height?

Alisson Becker is 6ft 4in tall and is one of the goalkeepers that rely a lot on their height to make amazing and crucial saves.

What position does Alisson Becker play?

Alisson Becker career

Alisson Becker is regarded to be one of the finest goalkeepers to ever play in the English Premier League. He has made a total of 383 appearances and scored a goal so far in his career.

He has been the Brazilian National Team’s wall since 2015. Alisson has made a total of 60 appearances for the Brazilian team.

He was part of the Brazil squad that won the Copa América in 2019.

Alisson has won the English Premier League, FA Cup, EFL Cup, and the Champions League so far with Liverpool. He has also won the Best FIFA Goalkeeper Award, the Yashin Trophy, Premier League Golden Glove amongst other honors.

Who is Alisson Becker’s wife?

Alisson Becker is married to Natália Loewe. The two started dating in 2012 and decided to tie the knot in 2015.
They have been immensely in love with each other and have been the pillars of each other’s lives.

Who are Alisson Becker’s parents?

It is quite unfortunate that Alisson Becker lost his father not long ago.
José Agostinho Becker was 57 years old when he passed away. He was reported missing for a while until the police discovered his body in a lake.

Magali Lino de Souza Becker is the mother of Alisson Becker. She has kept almost everything about her life private and it is said that she is still grieving the loss of her husband.

Who are Alisson Becker’s children?

Alisson Becker and Natália Loewe have welcomed 3 children into the world. The name of the kids is Helena, Rafael, and Matteo Becker.
