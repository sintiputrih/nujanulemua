A stand-up comedian, podcaster, actress and television, Nikki Glaser is best known for hosting her eponymous show, Not Safe with Nikki Glaser. Since kicking off her stand-up career at the age of 18, Glaser has touched commendable heights in the industry.  

Born on June 1, 1984, in Cincinnati, Ohio, Nikki got her breakthrough, performing stand-up on The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, Conan. Is Nikki Glaser Married?  

After decades of portraying her funny side to fans, Glaser adapted to a fresh change when she opened her personal life for fans in 2022. The 2022 reality show, Welcome Home Nikki Glaser? on E! showed Nikki being her real self with her family, and then-boyfriend, Chris Convoy, in her hometown of St. Louis.   

The pandemic-filmed reality show ignited a slowly swirling marriage rumor since Glaser was spending more time with Chris, who she had dated on-and-off for eight years. Another reason further fueling the rumor was Nikki's comedy career that had took a blow during the lockdown period.  

The reality show, which also included Glaser's parents, Julie and EJ, bestie Kerstin Robertson, and dog Walker, followed Glaser's conflicted love life with Convoy. The comedian-turned-host admitted her desire to settle down and also expressed her dismay over Convoy's unsure disposition.  

"I want so badly to be able to say, 'Let's just f--king do it and get married," Glaser said on the reality show, adding, "We've been doing this off and on for eight years. Like, what are we doing?'"  

Although the pair didn't get hitched or engaged on the show, the closest thing to it that Convoy could pull was getting down on one knee and asking Nikki to be his "emergency contact." "Hey, you got to start somewhere. It might not look like it, but this moment is huge for us," said the comedian.   

The Truth About Nikki Glaser and Chris Convoy's Relationship!  

Fans are certainly taking more interest in the Glaser's love life after seeing her loosen up more in her eponymous reality show. The podcast host hasn't revealed much about her dating experience but her 2022 E! show has divulged just enough about her relationship with Chris.

The comedian even dished on sharing workspace with her then-beau. "He is such a fantastic person to work with and he has made my show so great. I would never fire him," Nikki shared. Although Nikki is yet to confirm it, the pair are seemingly still working on their relationship.
