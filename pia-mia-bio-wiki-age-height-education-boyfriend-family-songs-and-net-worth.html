    Pia started her career by uploading videos of herself singing on the video-sharing site YouTube and went on to star in commercials and music videos. She was featured on will. i.am’s single Boys & Girls reached the top forty in the UK Singles Chart in 2016. She independently released an EP named The Gift 2 in the year 2017.
