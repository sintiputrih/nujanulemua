Kylie Jenner Shares Photos of Stormi and Aire for Mothers Day 2023

March 2024 · 2 minute read

Kylie Jenner celebrated Mother’s Day by sharing new adorable photos of her two kids, one-year-old son Aire and five-year-old daughter Stormi.

In her latest Instagram post, the Kylie Cosmetics mogul offered a glimpse into her life as a mom of two. The pictures show Aire (wearing adorable dinosaur Posh Peanut pajamas) and Stormi (in a Hello Kitty x Posh peanut PJ set) playing together, while others show the trio cuddling up close.

The images also captured sweet mother-and-son moments, like Jenner cuddling Aire close to her while lounging poolside in a black bikini, and pressing her forehead against Aire’s while the two have a meal by the pool.

Another snap shows three generations of Jenner’s family, with Aire posing alongside Jenner’s mom, Kris, and grandmother Mary Jo Campbell.

“Happy mother’s day 🫶🏻,” Jenner captioned the post.

Instagram

She shares Aire and Stormi with her ex-boyfriend, rapper Travis Scott.

The reality TV star is known to occasionally highlight her kids on social media, especially as they achieve new milestones. For instance, while celebrating Aire’s first birthday and Stormi’s fifth birthday earlier this year, Jenner dedicated tributes to her children on Instagram.

“I gave you the gift of life and life gave me the gift of you. the most special girl. this little face. i will miss it as it keeps changing. 5 years of loving you and forever more to go. i will always be there for you storm girl,” she wrote for Stormi’s birthday.

For Aire’s big day, she wrote, “AIRE. my son, my moon, my stars. Best year of my life with you. you complete us my angel. mommy loves you. happy 1st birthday. may God always bless you.”
